The Proprietor

Mr. kwasi Okyere is the proprietor of Kumasi Best Brain International School. Married to Mrs. Jennifer Frimpong for 21 years and still counting, God has blessed them with three beautiful children namely; Akua Anima Okyere, Akua Amoakowaa Okyere and Kwasi Okyere Jnr.

Mr Okyere is a very good Christian (Jehovah’s Witness) who upholds virtues and frowns on all social vices and he believes in the teachings and advices laid out in the Holy Bible. A very affable, Godfearing loving and kind hearted man who is appreciated by all.

He is also a business oriented man who strives to achieve the targets he sets for himself.

‘’Never leave Common Sense out in any of your doings. Apply it in all your spheres of Life’’ By Kwasi Okyere, 2021.

The Administrator

My name is Clement Kwame Nyame, the administrator of Kumasi Best Brain International School

This institution started in 2004 with humble beginning and today it has grown to a standard amongst the Suame Municipality As an admin, I have a major role to play for the institution to grow, most of my duties include the smooth running of the facility and staff, overseers the day to day functions of School at every level.

I provide leadership in times of crisis and lay out optimistic visions for the future. As a supervisor and accountant I must ensure that all staff are well cared for. In terms of academics, I commend all and sundry who helped in divisive ways for this institution to raise to this height.

I cannot overemphasized the Proprietor and his family their good intention for this School in the Abouhia community. He did not take School fees since inception up-to –date.

I recalled one of the academic year, a Military officer brought his ward and registered him, but upon learning that we do not take school fees he said “we are not serious how can we cater for teaching and learning”, he withdrawn his ward.

I strongly recommend this place for all parents and guardian who are looking for better Education for life.
